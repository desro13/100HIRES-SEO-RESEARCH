# 100Hires SEO Research

so I did a research on AI SEO for my 100Hires application.

## what i did

I looked at 10 experts on YouTube who teach about AI and SEO and stuff. I got some transcripts of their videos and also looked at their LinkedIn.

## the experts

i found these 10 people:

1. Nathan Gotch - he talks about SEO and AI
2. Ahrefs - they have a channel about SEO tools
3. Neil Patel - famous SEO guy
4. Eric Siu - has a podcast about marketing
5. Surfer SEO - tool for SEO content
6. Joanna Wiebe - she writes copy for websites
7. Greg Isenberg - makes AI stuff for marketing
8. Tim the SEO Guru - uses Claude for SEO
9. SE Ranking - another SEO tool company
10. Nico AI Ranking - does AI SEO stuff

## what i learned

AI is changing SEO i think. people are using chatgpt more than google now. some experts say you need to update your content every month or AI wont cite you.
