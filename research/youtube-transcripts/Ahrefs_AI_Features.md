# Ahrefs_AI_Features

**Expert:** Ahrefs (Sam Oh)
**Video Topic:** New Ahrefs AI Features for SEO
**Source:** youtube.com/@AhrefsCom

## Summary

Overview of Ahrefs' AI-powered features including their AI Content Grader, AI Overview tracking, and brand mention monitoring tools. Sam Oh demonstrates how these tools help SEO professionals adapt to the AI search landscape.

## Key Points

- **AI Content Grader**: Ahrefs' tool evaluates content against AI citation criteria — structure, freshness, authority signals, and question relevance. Provides a score and specific recommendations.
- **Brand Radar**: Monitors brand mentions across AI search results. Shows which AI assistants are citing your brand and which competitors are winning AI visibility.
- **AI Overview Tracking**: Tracks how Google AI Overviews feature your content, showing impression and click-through data specifically for AI-generated snippets.
- **Content Gap Analysis**: Identifies questions your competitors answer in AI results that your content doesn't cover — creating a tactical content roadmap.

## Practical Takeaways

- Use content grading tools to evaluate AI-readiness before publishing
- Monitor brand mentions across all major AI assistants
- Identify and close content gaps that competitors are winning in AI search
- Track AI Overview performance separately from traditional SERP performance
