# NathanGotch_Claude_Ranking

**Expert:** Nathan Gotch
**Video Topic:** Claude Ranking — How to Get Cited in Claude AI Responses
**Source:** youtube.com/@nathangotch

## Summary

Gotch explores the differences between optimizing for Claude vs. other AI assistants. Claude has different citation patterns and preferences compared to ChatGPT or Gemini.

## Key Points

- **Claude's Citation Style**: Claude tends to cite longer-form, well-structured content compared to ChatGPT's preference for concise snippets. Pages with 2,000+ words of authoritative content get cited more.
- **Source Preference**: Claude favors established domains with clear authorship (E-E-A-T signals). Content without bylines or author attribution is significantly less likely to be cited.
- **Technical Focus**: Claude is more likely to cite content with technical depth — data, statistics, code examples, and specific methodologies.
- **Claude + Search**: Claude's browsing capability is more conservative than ChatGPT's — it cites fewer sources but from more authoritative domains. Quality over quantity.

## Practical Takeaways

- Include author bylines and credentials on all content
- Add data-driven depth (statistics, case studies, methodologies)
- Write comprehensive (2,000+ word) content for Claude optimization
- Build domain authority through consistent publication on a focused topic
