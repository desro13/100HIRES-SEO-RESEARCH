# Ahrefs_AI_SEO_Course

**Expert:** Ahrefs (Sam Oh)
**Video Topic:** Complete AI SEO Course for Beginners
**Source:** youtube.com/@AhrefsCom

## Summary

A comprehensive 1-hour 26-minute course covering everything from how AI search engines work to exact tactics for getting cited, mentioned, and recommended across all major AI platforms.

## Key Points

- **Four Module Structure**:
  - Module 1: How AI search works — training data vs. real-time retrieval, query fan-out mechanics
  - Module 2: Strategy — brand gap analysis, keyword + prompt research, separating SEO from AEO opportunities
  - Module 3: Execution — content that gets cited, earning third-party mentions, YouTube optimization for AI, technical fixes for AI crawlers
  - Module 4: Measurement — AI referral tracking in GA4, bot analytics, re-auditing brand gaps
- **Word Count Myth Debunked**: Word count barely matters for AI citation. What matters: structure, freshness, format, and authority signals.
- **YouTube for AI Overviews**: Video content optimized for YouTube can appear in Google AI Overviews — creating a dual visibility channel.

## Practical Takeaways

- Work through the four-module structure as a complete AI SEO playbook
- Don't focus on word count — focus on structure and freshness
- Optimize YouTube content as part of AI SEO strategy
- Set up proper AI referral tracking in analytics before launching campaigns
