# NeilPatel_ChatGPT_Playbook

**Expert:** Neil Patel
**Video Topic:** The ChatGPT SEO Playbook
**Source:** youtube.com/@neilpatel

## Summary

Neil Patel's tactical playbook for optimizing content to appear in ChatGPT's responses. Covers specific strategies, content formats, and technical considerations.

## Key Points

- **Q&A Format Optimization**: ChatGPT frequently pulls from FAQ sections, "People Also Ask" content, and structured question-answer pairs. Creating dedicated FAQ content is the fastest path to ChatGPT citations.
- **Conversational Language**: ChatGPT prefers content written in natural, conversational language over formal or academic writing. Write like you're answering a friend's question.
- **Recent Publication Date**: ChatGPT's browsing mode shows a strong preference for content published or updated within the last 3 months. Fresh content has a significant advantage.
- **Multiple Format Coverage**: Cover your topic in multiple formats — blog posts, videos, infographics, podcasts — to increase the surface area for ChatGPT to find and cite you.

## Practical Takeaways

- Create dedicated FAQ and Q&A content on your site
- Write in conversational, natural language
- Keep content fresh — update or add new content every 90 days
- Cover topics across multiple content formats
