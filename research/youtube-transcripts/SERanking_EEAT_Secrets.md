# SERanking_EEAT_Secrets

**Expert:** SE Ranking
**Video Topic:** E-E-A-T Secrets for AI Search
**Source:** youtube.com/@se_ranking

## Summary

SE Ranking explores how Google's E-E-A-T (Experience, Expertise, Authoritativeness, Trustworthiness) framework applies to AI-powered search — and how to optimize for it.

## Key Points

- **E-E-A-T in AI Search**: AI search engines use E-E-A-T signals to filter and rank content. Pages with demonstrable author expertise and authoritative citations are prioritized.
- **Experience Signals**: First-hand experience (product reviews, personal case studies, original research) is the strongest E-E-A-T differentiator in 2026. AI cannot fabricate genuine experience.
- **Expertise Signals**: Author credentials, professional affiliations, published works, and industry recognition. Content with named, credentialed authors outperforms anonymous or brand-only content.
- **Authoritativeness Signals**: External recognition — links from authoritative domains, mentions from industry leaders, citations in reputable publications.
- **Trustworthiness Signals**: Accuracy, transparency (about affiliations, conflicts of interest, methodology), and consistent factual correctness.

## Practical Takeaways

- Feature real, named authors with credentials on all content
- Include first-hand experience and original research
- Build external authority through earned mentions and links
- Be transparent about methodology, sources, and affiliations
- Fact-check rigorously — AI search penalizes inaccuracies heavily
