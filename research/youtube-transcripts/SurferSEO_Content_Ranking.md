# SurferSEO_Content_Ranking

**Expert:** Surfer SEO (Matt Kenyon)
**Video Topic:** How to Rank Content in 2026 — 5 Case Studies
**Source:** youtube.com/@SurferSEO

## Summary

Matt Kenyon presents 5 real case studies of content that ranked well in both traditional search and AI results — analyzing what each did right.

## Key Points

- **Case 1: The Fresh Content Win**: A site that updated its oldest content (2+ years old) with fresh data, new examples, and current statistics saw a 340% increase in AI citations within 30 days.
- **Case 2: Structure Overhaul**: A content piece that was rewritten with question-format headers and clear answer sections went from 0 to featured in ChatGPT responses within 2 weeks.
- **Case 3: Entity-First Strategy**: A brand that built strong entity signals (Wikipedia, Crunchbase, Google Knowledge Panel) saw 3x more AI citations than competitors with better content but weaker entity presence.
- **Case 4: Multi-Format Coverage**: A topic covered in blog, video, and infographic formats appeared in 60% more AI responses than competitors covering it in blog-only format.
- **Case 5: The E-E-A-T Effect**: Adding named author bios with credentials and external citations to an existing article increased AI citations by 180%.

## Practical Takeaways

- Refresh old content for immediate AI visibility gains
- Use question-format headers for structured content
- Build entity presence beyond your website
- Cover topics in multiple content formats
- Show author credentials and cite external sources
