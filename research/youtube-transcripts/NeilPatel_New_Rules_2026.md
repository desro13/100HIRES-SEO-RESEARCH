# NeilPatel_New_Rules_2026

**Expert:** Neil Patel
**Video Topic:** The New Rules of SEO in 2026
**Source:** youtube.com/@neilpatel

## Summary

Neil Patel updates his SEO framework for the AI era, explaining what still works, what's changed, and what's new.

## Key Points

- **What Still Works**: Quality content, backlinks (from relevant sources), technical SEO fundamentals (site speed, mobile optimization, proper indexing), and user experience signals.
- **What's Changed**: Keyword density is dead. Exact-match keyword targeting has been replaced by topic relevance and entity coverage. Single-page optimization is less important than site-wide topical authority.
- **What's New**: AI citation optimization, E-E-A-T signal building, entity association management, and multi-surface visibility (Google + ChatGPT + Perplexity + social).
- **The AI-Proof Strategy**: Build an authoritative brand, create genuinely useful content, earn links and citations, and maintain technical excellence. The fundamentals haven't changed — the execution has.

## Practical Takeaways

- Don't abandon traditional SEO — it still works
- Shift from keyword targeting to topic coverage
- Build entity associations beyond your website
- Start optimizing for AI search surfaces alongside traditional SEO
- The fundamentals (quality, authority, user experience) are more important than ever
