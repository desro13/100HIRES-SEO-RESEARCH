# NicoAIRanking_Perplexity_Research

**Expert:** Nico AI Ranking
**Video Topic:** Using Perplexity for SEO Research
**Source:** youtube.com/@Nico_AIRanking

## Summary

Nico shows how to use Perplexity as an SEO research tool — not just as a search engine to optimize for, but as a research assistant to uncover content opportunities.

## Key Points

- **Perplexity as a Research Tool**: Perplexity's cited-sources format reveals exactly which content AI values for a given topic. Use this as a competitive intelligence tool.
- **Gap Analysis with Perplexity**: Ask Perplexity about your topic and note which sources it cites. If your content isn't there, that's a content gap you can fill.
- **Question Discovery**: Perplexity's follow-up questions reveal the "question ecosystem" around a topic. These are content opportunities that traditional keyword tools miss.
- **Perplexity vs. Google**: Perplexity's citation patterns differ significantly from Google's SERP. Content that performs well in Perplexity may not rank on Google — and vice versa.

## Practical Takeaways

- Use Perplexity as an SEO research and competitive intelligence tool
- Identify your content gaps by checking if Perplexity cites your brand
- Mine Perplexity's suggested questions for content ideas
- Optimize for Perplexity separately from Google — they're different systems
