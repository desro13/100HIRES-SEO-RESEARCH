# Ahrefs_Content_Writing

**Expert:** Ahrefs (Sam Oh)
**Video Topic:** Content Writing for SEO — Best Practices
**Source:** youtube.com/@AhrefsCom

## Summary

Sam Oh covers the fundamentals of writing content that ranks in both traditional search and AI-powered discovery. Focuses on process, structure, and quality signals.

## Key Points

- **The Briefing Process**: Start with a content brief that covers target audience, search intent, related questions, and competitor analysis. AI-optimized content starts with better planning.
- **Writing for Humans First**: Content written primarily for search engines rarely performs well in AI citations. Write for humans, then optimize structure for AI parsing.
- **Evidence & Citations**: Linking to authoritative sources increases your own authority. Content with 3+ external citations to respected domains performs better in AI search.
- **Readability**: AI search engines prefer content at a grade 8–10 reading level. Complex academic language gets cited less frequently than clear, accessible writing.

## Practical Takeaways

- Create detailed content briefs before writing
- Write for humans first, optimize structure for AI second
- Include external citations to authoritative sources
- Target grade 8–10 reading level for maximum AI citation potential
