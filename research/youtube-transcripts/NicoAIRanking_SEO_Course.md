**Expert:** Nico AI Ranking

## Summary

Nico's complete SEO curriculum for 2026 — how he'd approach learning SEO from scratch in the AI era, based on 10+ years of experience.

## Key Points

- **Step 1: Choose a Website**: Start with a real website in a real niche. Learning SEO theory without practice is ineffective.
- **Step 2: SEO Fundamentals**: Master the basics — keyword research, on-page optimization, technical SEO, link building. AI hasn't changed these fundamentals.
- **Step 3: Niche Down**: Deep expertise in a specific niche beats general SEO knowledge. AI favors specialized authorities over generalists.
- **Step 4: Human & AI Optimization**: Optimize for both human readers and AI citation simultaneously. They're not separate strategies.
- **Step 5: Video SEO**: YouTube is the #2 search engine and a major source of AI citations. Video content is underutilized in most SEO strategies.
- **Step 6: AI Operators**: Learn to use AI tools (Claude, ChatGPT, Perplexity) as SEO operators — they can analyze data, generate content, and monitor performance.
- **Step 7: The Loop**: Create → measure → optimize → repeat. Consistent iteration beats one-time optimization every time.

## Practical Takeaways

- Start with fundamentals before AI optimization
- Develop deep niche expertise
- Add video SEO to your strategy
- Learn to use AI assistants as SEO operators
- Implement the continuous improvement loop
