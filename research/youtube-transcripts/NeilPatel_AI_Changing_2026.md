# NeilPatel_AI_Changing_2026

**Expert:** Neil Patel
**Video Topic:** How AI Is Changing SEO in 2026
**Source:** youtube.com/@neilpatel

## Summary

Neil Patel's analysis of AI's impact on search in 2026 — covering Google AI Overviews, ChatGPT as a search competitor, and what brands need to do differently.

## Key Points

- **ChatGPT as a Search Competitor**: ChatGPT has become a significant traffic source. Neil cites data showing ChatGPT referrals now rival organic search for some industries.
- **AI Overviews Reduce Clicks**: Google AI Overviews answer queries directly on the SERP, reducing click-through rates. Brands need to focus on "being the answer" rather than "getting the click."
- **Long-Tail Opportunity**: As AI handles simple queries, traditional SEO is shifting to long-tail, complex, comparison-based searches where AI still defers to human-readable content.
- **Brand Building is SEO**: As AI citations favor authoritative brands, building brand recognition outside of search (social media, PR, partnerships) directly impacts AI search visibility.

## Practical Takeaways

- Accept that click-through rates will decline — optimize for answer inclusion
- Target complex, comparison-based queries for traditional SEO
- Invest in brand building as an SEO strategy
- Track AI referral traffic separately from organic traffic
