# EricSiu_Google_Changed

**Expert:** Eric Siu
**Video Topic:** How Google Changed SEO Forever
**Source:** youtube.com/@LevelingUpOfficial

## Summary

Eric Siu analyzes Google's transformation from a 10-blue-links search engine to an AI-powered answer engine, and what this means for marketers and agency owners.

## Key Points

- **Zero-Click Searches**: With AI Overviews and AI Mode, more searches result in zero clicks. The value is shifting from "getting the click" to "being the answer."
- **The AI Traffic Trade-Off**: Brands may get fewer clicks but higher-quality traffic from AI referrals. At Single Grain, AI-referred visitors convert at higher rates even though volume is lower.
- **Agency Business Model Shift**: SEO agencies need to shift from "rankings reporting" to "visibility reporting" — showing clients where they appear across AI surfaces.
- **Content Quality Renaissance**: Google's AI updates have actually improved content quality. Thin, keyword-stuffed content is being devalued across both traditional and AI search.

## Practical Takeaways

- Accept that zero-click searches are the new normal
- Measure AI visibility, not just keyword rankings
- Shift agency/client reporting to include AI surface coverage
- Invest in genuine content quality — it's rewarded across all search surfaces
