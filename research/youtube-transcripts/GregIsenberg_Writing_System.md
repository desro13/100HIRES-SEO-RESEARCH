# GregIsenberg_Writing_System

**Expert:** Greg Isenberg
**Video Topic:** My AI Writing System for Business Content
**Source:** youtube.com/@GregIsenberg

## Summary

Greg shares his personal AI writing workflow — a system he uses daily to produce startup content, newsletters, and SEO content. Focuses on efficiency without sacrificing quality.

## Key Points

- **The Two-Pass Method**: First pass: AI generates raw content from brief and sources (speed, volume). Second pass: human edits for voice, nuance, and accuracy (quality, authenticity). Never publish AI-only content.
- **Source-First Approach**: Before asking AI to write, Greg inputs 5–10 source links for context. AI writes better when grounded in real references.
- **The Voice Layer**: After content is written, a separate AI pass analyzes and adjusts the text to match his speaking/writing style. This preserves brand voice.
- **Distribution Automation**: The same system repurposes content across LinkedIn, Twitter, newsletter, and blog — each with platform-specific formatting.

## Practical Takeaways

- Never skip the human editing pass
- Provide source links before generating content
- Add a voice-matching layer after writing
- Build platform-specific distribution into your workflow
