# JoannaWiebe_SEO_Copywriting

**Expert:** Joanna Wiebe
**Video Topic:** SEO Copywriting — Writing Content That Ranks on Google
**Source:** youtube.com/@joanna-wiebe

## Summary

Joanna's framework for SEO copywriting: content structured to rank in search engines while compelling human readers to take action. The intersection of information architecture and persuasion.

## Key Points

- **Structure for Scanners**: Online readers don't read — they scan. Content must be structured for scannability: compelling headers, bullet points, bold key phrases, short paragraphs.
- **The Inverted Pyramid**: Start with the most important information (the answer), then provide supporting details, then background context. This satisfies both AI search (which extracts answers) and human readers (who want quick answers).
- **Emotional Hooks in SEO Content**: Traditional SEO content is emotionally flat. Adding emotional hooks — curiosity, urgency, aspiration — improves both engagement and conversion.
- **CTA as Part of the Content**: Every piece of content should have a clear next step for the reader. Not "click here" but a natural progression to deeper content or an offer.

## Practical Takeaways

- Structure content for scanners first, deep readers second
- Lead with the answer, then provide depth
- Add emotional hooks — SEO content doesn't have to be boring
- Include a natural next step in every piece of content
