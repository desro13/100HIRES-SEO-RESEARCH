# NathanGotch_AEO_Guide

**Expert:** Nathan Gotch
**Video Topic:** AEO (Answer Engine Optimization) Guide
**Source:** youtube.com/@nathangotch

## Summary

Nathan Gotch explains how AI search — specifically ChatGPT, Perplexity, Google AI Overviews, and Gemini — has created a new optimization layer called Answer Engine Optimization (AEO). He argues that SEO changed more in the last 12 months than in the previous 10 years combined.

## Key Points

- **AEO vs. SEO**: Traditional SEO optimizes for 10 blue links; AEO optimizes for direct answers in AI-generated responses. Rankings don't exist in the same way — instead, brands compete for citations and mentions.
- **Question Targeting**: Instead of keyword research (queries people type), AEO requires question research (queries people ask). Long-tail questions that AI assistants can answer concisely perform best.
- **Structured Content**: AI search engines prefer content with clear headers, bullet points, definitions, and Q&A sections. Unstructured prose is less likely to be cited.
- **Authority Signals**: Being cited by other authoritative sources increases AI citation probability. Gotch recommends a "citation building" strategy parallel to traditional link building.
- **The 30-Day Refresh Rule**: Content older than 30 days without updates sees a significant drop in AI citation rates (corroborating Ahrefs' 76% finding).

## Practical Takeaways

- Restructure existing content to include explicit Q&A sections
- Target questions, not just keywords
- Refresh content within 30 days of publication
- Build citations from authoritative sources (not just links)
- Monitor AI citation using dedicated tools
