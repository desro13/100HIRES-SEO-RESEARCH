# SERanking_AI_Content

**Expert:** SE Ranking
**Video Topic:** How AI Content Kills Your SEO (16-Month Experiment)
**Source:** youtube.com/@se_ranking

## Summary

SE Ranking presents results from a 16-month experiment on how AI-generated content (without human oversight) impacts SEO performance. The findings challenge assumptions about AI content at scale.

## Key Points

- **The Experiment**: SE Ranking published AI-generated content across multiple sites over 16 months, tracking rankings, traffic, and AI citations over time.
- **Initial Results**: AI content performed well for the first 3–6 months — ranking for long-tail keywords and generating traffic.
- **The Crash**: After 6–9 months, most AI-only sites saw significant ranking drops. Google's algorithm updates identified and devalued low-effort AI content.
- **What Worked**: AI content with significant human editing — restructuring, fact-checking, adding original insights — maintained and improved rankings over the full 16 months.
- **The Threshold**: Content that was >70% AI-generated underperformed. Content with <40% AI generation (mostly human-written with AI assistance) performed best.

## Practical Takeaways

- Don't publish AI content without human editorial oversight
- The AI content threshold is about 40% — mostly human with AI assistance
- Long-term SEO requires genuine value, not just AI volume
- AI content can work as a starting point but needs significant human revision
