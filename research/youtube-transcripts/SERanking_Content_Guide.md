**Expert:** SE Ranking

**Source:** youtube.com/@se_ranking

## Summary

SE Ranking's comprehensive guide to content optimization — covering both traditional SEO requirements and new AI-readiness criteria.

## Key Points

- **Content Silos**: Organize content into topical silos (clusters) around pillar pages. This signals authority to both Google and AI search engines.
- **Internal Linking Structure**: Proper internal linking between silo pages distributes authority and helps AI crawlers understand topic relationships.
- **Multimedia Integration**: Content with images, videos, charts, and infographics ranks better and gets more AI citations than text-only content.
- **Schema Markup**: Implementing proper schema (especially FAQ, HowTo, Article, and Organization) increases the chance of AI citation by helping AI systems parse content structure.
- **Mobile-First**: With Google's mobile-first indexing and the rise of AI-powered mobile search, mobile optimization is table stakes.

## Practical Takeaways

- Build topical silos around pillar pages
- Use strategic internal linking within content clusters
- Add relevant images, videos, and data visualizations
- Implement schema markup (FAQ, HowTo, Article)
- Ensure flawless mobile experience
