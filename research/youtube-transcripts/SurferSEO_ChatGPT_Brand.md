# SurferSEO_ChatGPT_Brand

**Expert:** Surfer SEO (Matt Kenyon)
**Video Topic:** ChatGPT, Claude & Gemini Built My SEO Strategy — Here's What Actually Worked
**Source:** youtube.com/@SurferSEO

## Summary

Matt Kenyon shares a case study of using AI assistants (ChatGPT, Claude, Gemini) to build an SEO strategy — and which AI-generated recommendations actually drove results.

## Key Points

- **AI as Strategy Assistant**: Use different AI assistants for different strategic tasks — ChatGPT for content ideas, Claude for technical analysis, Gemini for data synthesis.
- **The Verification Step**: All AI-generated recommendations must be verified against real data (analytics, search console, ranking data). AI often suggests things that sound good but don't work.
- **What Actually Worked**: AI recommendations for content restructuring, question-answer formatting, and internal linking consistently drove improvements. Generic "write more content" recommendations didn't.
- **The Debate Feature**: Asking multiple AI assistants the same strategic question and comparing answers reveals blind spots and generates better strategy than relying on one AI.

## Practical Takeaways

- Use multiple AI assistants for complementary strategic input
- Always verify AI recommendations against real performance data
- Focus on AI suggestions about structure and format — they work
- Cross-reference strategic advice across different AI models
