**Expert:** Nathan Gotch
**Video Topic:** How to Dominate AI Search in 2026
**Source:** youtube.com/@nathangotch

## Summary

A comprehensive strategic overview of dominating AI-powered search across multiple platforms — Google AI Overviews, ChatGPT, Perplexity, Gemini, and Claude. Gotch presents his complete AI search operating system.

## Key Points

- **The AI Search Stack**: Brands need to optimize across 5 AI search surfaces: Google AI Overviews, ChatGPT (both training data and browsing), Perplexity, Gemini, and Claude. Each has different citation preferences.
- **Brand-as-Answer Strategy**: Instead of ranking for keywords, brands should aim to be "the answer" for their category. This means owning the narrative and having the most authoritative content on a topic.
- **Content Depth Over Volume**: Publishing 1 comprehensive, authoritative page is worth more than 10 thin pages optimized for different keywords. AI search favors depth.
- **Entity Building**: Creating strong entity associations (Google Knowledge Panel, Wikipedia, Crunchbase, etc.) increases AI citation probability by up to 3x.
- **The Feedback Loop**: Monitor AI citations → identify content gaps → create/update content → monitor again. This iterative cycle is essential for maintaining AI visibility.

## Practical Takeaways

- Optimize for all 5 major AI search surfaces, not just one
- Build entity associations beyond your website
- Prioritize content depth over content volume
- Implement a monitoring → gap analysis → creation cycle
- Position your brand as the definitive answer in your niche
